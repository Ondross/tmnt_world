How to Play

Click the map to pause/unpause

All you gotta do is mine for energy, and then use that energy to blast the other turtles! You're Leo, the blue one.
Tell Leo what to do by writing code for his turtle brain. Every time you hit submit, Leo will follow the instructions in the box. It's not case-sensitive.

Here is some of the stuff you can do:
chase(Anything): Chase after something
evade(Anything): Run away
shoot(Anything): Kill stuff. Bullets have a range and they cost energy.
mine(Planet): Mine a planet to get it's energy, dude! (You will automatically move towards a planet if you mine it)

Things in the world:
Turtles: Ralph, Donna, Mitch, closest_turtle. The array is called Turtles
Bullets: closest_bullet, or the array Bullets.
Planets: Mars, Mercury, closest_planet, or the array Planets.
All these guys have attributes you can check out, like health, energy, etc. Hovering gives you the basic ones. Use the console to check out what else is available.

Example Code:
chase(Mitch);
mine(Mars);
evade(Donna);
shoot(Ralph);
evade(closest_bullet);
chase(closest_turtle);

You can get more complicated:
if (distance_to(closest_turtle) < 200){ shoot(closest_turtle); }
